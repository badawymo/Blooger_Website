keyword research

Keyword research is the backbone of search engine optimization. It helps you to find out which words and phrases people are using to search for your business. Knowing what keywords people are searching for can help you to optimize your website and content so that they appear in the top ten results on Google, Bing, or Yahoo. Keyword research also helps you understand how people are searching for your business so that you can create content specifically tailored to their needs
